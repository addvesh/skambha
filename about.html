DISCOVER who we are !

Manav Samajik Seva Sansthan is a Not for Profit organisation registered under Section 21 of Registration of Societies Act - 1860. Registered on 06/07/2013, the organization aims at reducing Social stigmas related to Education, healthcare and skill development. The organization has taken the initiative to bring transformational changes in the rural areas of Uttar Pradesh by providing free tuitions to all age group, spreading awareness about basic medication and helping the people access the basic services of healthcare, education and skill development.Having the capacity and zeal to work for the society we have devoted ourselves for the mankind. We have been assessed and are complaint with the requirements of ISO 9001:2015. Being an active and answerable organization organization we are also compliant with the requirements of Section 12AB of IT Act 2020.
